US National Guard Contract

The Challenge

The US National Guard presented a unique opportunity to expand into the Government/Defense vertical—a market with zero brand recognition for our services. We faced a "black box" procurement cycle with heavy resistance from Legal, Security, and Finance teams.

Strategic Approach

  • Value Translation: Translated complex FINRA regulations into clear business-value frameworks for government context.
  • Stakeholder Mapping: Identified and aligned key decision-makers across procurement and JAG (Judge Advocate General).
  • Direct Engagement: Personally facilitated every security review session to ensure internal compliance aligned with government auditors.

Results & Impact

  • Secured landmark multi-year contract
  • Established repeatable playbook for defense-sector expansion

Key Learning (Honesty)

"I initially underestimated the security audit timeline by 4 months, nearly stalling the deal. I learned to build a mandatory 30% 'compliance buffer' into all government sales cycles moving forward."

14-State Territory Launch

The Challenge

Tasked with scaling sales operations from a single territory (California) to 14 US states with no existing brand presence. Prospects in the Midwest had zero awareness of our West Coast successes, and regulatory environments varied by state.

Strategic Approach

  • Risk & Control Pivot: Shifted from feature-selling to a consultative model focusing on regional regulatory compliance as the primary trust builder.
  • RevOps Implementation: Enforced strict CRM hygiene to track deal velocity across disparate territories.
  • Localized Vernacular: Adapted sales scripts to match regional business languages.

Results & Impact

  • Captured 600+ net new logos
  • Scaled AUM from $0 to $50M+ in under 6 years

Key Learning (Honesty)

"In the first two states, I used California scripts. They failed. I realized 'Expansion' requires translation. I spent 3 weeks shadowing local brokers to learn regional vernacular before securing our first win."
